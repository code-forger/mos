Welcome to MOS: A Simple Multitasking OS

MOS is an operating system designed to be simply, stable, versatile and fair.
It operates entirely in text mode graphics to give a simple yet powerful user interaction.
Shipping with a wide selection of common command line tools, MOS is ready to support your every day use.

To dive in and give it a try, go a head to jump to Installing / Emulating in the side bar.
